Mission

A cluster of meteors are coming towards the Earth and our orbital cannons cannot lock on the large meteors because of the meteorites surrounding them. Commander, you are tasked to fly the prototype spaceship equipped with ion cannons and destroy smaller meteorites so that our orbital cannons can precisely clear the main threats. Manually targeting large meteors can also make our job down here easier. Fate of humanity depends on you...

Gameplay

Each opened box shows how many meteors are located around the perimeter. You must avoid opening a box which has a meteor inside. You can target certain boxes to make them uninteractable or you can mark them with a question mark if you are not totally sure box contains a mine. Game is lost when you open a box with meteor and it is won when you locate all non meteor boxes by clicking them. After the game ends, result is recorded to scoreboard for the current player.
